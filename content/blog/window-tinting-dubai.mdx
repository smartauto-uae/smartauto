## Why Home Window Tinting Matters in Dubai

Dubai's UV index regularly hits **11–12 out of 12** — classified as extreme. Without window film,
your home's glass lets in up to **80% of solar heat**, directly raising indoor temperatures
and driving up your air conditioning costs.

## Types of Residential Window Film

### Solar Control Film
Rejects up to 90% of solar heat while maintaining natural light. The best choice
for south and west-facing rooms.

### Privacy Film
One-way mirror effect — see out clearly, no one sees in during daylight.

### Safety Film
Holds glass together on impact. Recommended for ground-floor windows and children's rooms.

## How Much Does Window Tinting Cost in Dubai?

Residential window film in Dubai typically ranges from **AED 18–40 per sqft**
depending on film type and brand.
